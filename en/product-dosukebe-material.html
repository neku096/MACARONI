Product Details

An R18 skin material for giving VRChat avatar skin a glossier, more sensual look. It includes Substance Designer-made textures, materials, and a lilToon preset, making it useful when you want stronger skin shading for Unity avatar photography or video production.

Dosukebe Material is intended for VRChat skin material workflows. The BOOTH description presents it as a realistic and glossy skin material.

The setup is described as a liltoon preset workflow: select the avatar's current skin material, then choose DosukebeSkin from the skin presets in liltoon settings.

If the texture effect is too strong, adjust the Normal map strength. If redness appears too strong, adjust liltoon's shadow-related settings. The final look may vary depending on the avatar, skin material, lighting, and Unity settings, so check the BOOTH product description while adjusting.

Setup

  1. Prepare a Unity 2022 project with liltoon installed.
  2. Import the purchased asset into the Unity project.
  3. Select the avatar skin material currently in use.
  4. In liltoon settings, choose Preset, Skin, then DosukebeSkin.
  5. After applying it, adjust the Normal map strength or shadow-related settings if needed.

The BOOTH product page states that it supports Unity 2022 only and should be used in a project with liltoon installed.
