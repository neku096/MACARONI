Product Details

Solo Motion vol.2 is an R18 solo motion asset for users who want to bring a private scene into avatar photography or video production.

It follows the same series structure as vol.1 while presenting vol.2 as a separate variation with its own main image, BOOTH link, and product information.

It is intended to be checked and adjusted in Unity according to your avatar, outfit, and scene needs. Please confirm the exact supported avatars and included files on the BOOTH product page before purchasing.

Setup

  1. Prepare a Unity 2022 project with Modular Avatar installed.
  2. Import both the unitypackage for your purchased avatar and the Common unitypackage.
  3. Place the supported avatar prefab named MBM2_○○○ directly under the avatar in the Hierarchy.
  4. Place MAMST_Controller directly under the avatar as well.
  5. Confirm that both MBM2_○○○ and MAMST_Controller are present before testing.
  6. If MAMST_Controller is already installed from another Macaroni pose or motion product, do not add it again.

This product uses Modular Avatar to add animations and Expression Menu entries by placing prefabs under the avatar. It is different from manually registering regular anim files in an Animator, so please follow the BOOTH product page for detailed requirements and notes.

FAQ

Can I use it with a modified avatar?

It may work if the avatar is based on a supported model. However, body shape, chest, waist, outfit, scale, and other modifications may require position, contact, or facial expression adjustments.

Can I use it outside VRChat?

As a Unity motion asset, it can also be used for 3D game production, video production, and still image creation. Please also check the rules of the publishing platform, the avatar creator's terms, and the environment where it will be used.

Do I need Modular Avatar?

This product is intended for use in a Unity 2022 project with Modular Avatar installed. It uses supported avatar prefabs, so please confirm your project environment and included files before setup.

Are the avatar models included?

No. Avatar models are not included. Please obtain each avatar from its official page separately.

What should I check before purchasing?

First, confirm that the avatar you want to use is included in the supported avatar list. If you plan to use sound or particles, it is also recommended to check whether they work in your Unity or recording environment.
